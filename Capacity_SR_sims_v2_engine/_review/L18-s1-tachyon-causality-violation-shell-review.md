- **[physics] Return-signal→causal-loop model needs verification (candidate).** `ct3 = E2.ct − |E2.x|/v2` (L793) hard-codes the return as backward-in-time in S at speed v2 and gives "slower v₂ ⇒ deeper past" (v2=1.2c→ct3=−0.67, v2=5c→ct3=+0.6).
