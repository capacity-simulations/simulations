Simulation Info

This simulation visualizes a 1D Gaussian wave packet, a common model in quantum mechanics for a localized free particle.

  • x₀ (Center): The expectation value of position.
  • σ (Width): Determines the spread (uncertainty) in position.
  • k₀ (Wave Number): Determines the momentum and phase velocity of the packet.
