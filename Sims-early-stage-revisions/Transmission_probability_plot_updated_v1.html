About this simulation. Plot of quantum transmission probability T for a 1D symmetric rectangular barrier (height U, half-width a). Use T vs E for energy on the horizontal axis, or T vs k for wave number. Adjust barrier and mass with the parameters; the curve updates in real time. Hover the plot to read T, E, k and η/κ at any point.

Formula
E < U (tunneling):
T = 4k²η² / [4k²η²
  + (k²+η²)²sinh²(2ηa)]

E > U (above barrier):
T = 4k²κ² / [4k²κ²
  + (k²−κ²)²sin²(2κa)]

η = √(2m(U−E))
κ = √(2m(E−U))
E = k²/(2m)
