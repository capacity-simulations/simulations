About the simulation

This simulation visualizes a single-qubit spin state on the Bloch sphere and shows how projective measurements along the X, Y, and Z axes collapse the state.

Use the Measure Spin buttons to perform axis measurements, Set Custom State to enter amplitudes or presets, and Reset in the header to return to the default state. The wavefunction panel and probability bars update after each action.
