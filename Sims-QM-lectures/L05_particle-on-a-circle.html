About this simulation

A quantum particle on a ring (\(R=1\); natural units \(\hbar=1\), \(2m=1\)) in periodic eigenstates labeled by the integer \(n\).

Physics

Energy \(E_n \propto n^2\), with lecture notation \(\psi_n(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi R}}e^{inx/R}\). The plot around the ring uses the angular coordinate \(\phi=x/R\). For \(n\neq 0\), \(\pm n\) are degenerate in energy.

\[E_n = \frac{n^2\hbar^2}{2mR^2}, \quad \psi_n(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi R}} e^{inx/R}, \quad P(x)=|\psi_n(x)|^2=\frac{1}{2\pi R}\]
Visuals

Energy levels: ladder of states vs energy; the chosen \(n\) is highlighted.
Wavefunction: real (blue) and imaginary (green) parts vs \(\phi\), or the same data wrapped on the ring; optional \(| \psi |^2\) band (yellow). Panels can be maximized.

Controls

Quantum Number: slider for \(n\); degeneracy note below it. Display Mode: ring vs \(\phi\) plot toggle; Show Re part / Show Im part; Show Probability Density \(|\psi|^2\); Animate Time Evolution. Visuals: Time Speed slider and Lecture display toggle. Current State: ring radius, \(E_n\), wavelength, and the lecture equations. Top bar: Reset / Pause.
