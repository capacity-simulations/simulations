EC 04/02/26: The harmonic oscillator simulation was updated so the user now sets \(m\) and \(\omega\) directly, with the wavefunction width, energy spacing, turning points, and potential all recalculated consistently from the full harmonic-oscillator formulas rather than a spring-constant-only shortcut. The potential panel was simplified by removing the decorative state overlays, and the UI was cleaned up by replacing the old override-\(\omega\) control with a display-only animation-speed slider and removing the extra stats/time readouts. These changes were made to keep the simulation closer to the lecture notation and to make the plots more physically honest and easier to interpret.
