About this simulation

This simulation shows a quantum-mechanical plane wave incident on a one-dimensional potential barrier (or step). You can watch the wave function ψ(x, t) evolve in time, and see the real part, imaginary part, and probability density |ψ|².

Adjust the barrier height U, half-width a, and particle energy E. The bottom panel plots the transmission probability T(E) versus energy; the current energy is marked on the curve. For a rectangular barrier centered on the origin, T = 1 at resonance energies above U.
