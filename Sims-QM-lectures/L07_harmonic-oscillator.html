About Simulation

This simulation models a 1D Quantum Harmonic Oscillator in units where \(\hbar = 1\), with user-controlled mass \(m\) and angular frequency \(\omega\).

  • Harmonic Potential: Displays the parabolic potential \(V(x)=\tfrac{1}{2}m\omega^2x^2\) and the corresponding energy levels.
  • Wavefunction: Visualizes the real and imaginary parts of the time-dependent wavefunction ψ(x,t).
  • Probability Density: Shows |ψ(x,t)|², representing the particle's position probability distribution.

Use the right sidebar to adjust the quantum state n, mass m, and angular frequency \(\omega\) to observe how the eigenstate width and energy spacing change. The animation-speed control only changes how quickly the plotted phase advances on screen.
