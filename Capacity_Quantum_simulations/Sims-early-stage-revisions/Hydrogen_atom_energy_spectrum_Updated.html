Hydrogen Atom Emission Spectrum i

About the simulation

This simulation shows the emission and absorption spectrum of the hydrogen atom using the Bohr model. Energy levels follow En = −13.6/n² eV. Choose emission (electron drops to a lower level, photon emitted) or absorption (electron jumps to a higher level, photon absorbed). Trigger single transitions with the dropdowns or run the Lyman, Balmer, or Paschen series. The spectrum strip displays wavelengths from UV to IR on a log scale.
