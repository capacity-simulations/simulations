Hydrogen Atom Emission Spectrum i

About the simulation

Explore hydrogen spectral lines in the Bohr model, where energy levels follow En = −13.6/n² eV. Switch between emission (photon emitted) and absorption (photon absorbed) transitions.

Visuals
  • Left: quantized energy levels with transition arrows and labels.
  • Right: atom view with circular orbits and the moving electron.
  • Bottom: wavelength strip (UV, Visible, IR) showing spectral lines.
Controls
  • Set transition levels with From n and To n.
  • Toggle Emission/Absorption mode.
  • Use series buttons (Lyman, Balmer, Paschen) or run all transitions.
  • Adjust animation speed and clear recorded lines.
