About This Simulation

This simulation visualizes coherent states in a quantum harmonic oscillator - special quantum states that most closely mimic classical motion.

The Gaussian wave packet represents the probability density |Ψ(x,t)|², while the "ghost particle" follows the classical trajectory for comparison.

The coherent state width is given by:

When the width σ 0.71 equals σ₀, the packet oscillates without changing shape (Coherent State). For any other width (Squeezed States), the packet "breathes" at frequency 2ω.
