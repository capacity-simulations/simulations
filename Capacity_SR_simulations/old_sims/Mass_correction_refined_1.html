About the Simulation
This simulation demonstrates Einstein's Special Theory of Relativity and how mass increases as an object approaches the speed of light.

The blue spacecraft represents an object at rest with its rest mass (m₀).

The pink spacecraft shows how the same object's relativistic mass increases as its velocity increases.

Use the velocity slider to see how mass grows exponentially as you approach the speed of light (c). At 99% of c, mass increases by over 7 times!

This effect explains why no massive object can reach the speed of light — it would require infinite energy.
