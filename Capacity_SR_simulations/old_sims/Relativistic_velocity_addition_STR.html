📖 About the Simulation
In special relativity, velocities don't simply add. When two objects move toward each other, their relative velocity is given by:

v_rel = (v₁ + v₂) / (1 + v₁v₂/c²)

This ensures no relative velocity ever exceeds the speed of light (c), even if both objects travel at 99% c.
