About This Simulation

This interactive simulation demonstrates that scalar quantities remain unchanged when the coordinate system is rotated.

Two vectors A and B are shown in two frames: the original frame S and the rotated frame S′.

Vectors transform, but the scalars ‖A‖, ‖B‖, ‖A − B‖, A · B, and φ remain invariant under rotation. Here φ is the smaller angle between vectors A and B, and is undefined if either vector has zero length.

Drag vector tips on either panel to modify A and B. Use the θ slider to rotate the right frame.
