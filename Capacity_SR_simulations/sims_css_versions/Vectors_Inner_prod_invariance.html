Inner Product Invariance Under Rotation
i
About This Simulation
This simulation demonstrates that the inner product (dot product) of two vectors is invariant under rotation of the coordinate system.

Two vectors A and B are displayed in two side-by-side panels. The left panel shows the original reference frame (x, y), while the right panel shows a rotated frame (x', y') whose axes are tilted by angle θ.

Although the components of each vector change when expressed in the rotated frame, the dot product A · B = AxBx + AyBy remains the same in both frames.

Drag the vector tips to reposition them. Use the θ slider in the control panel to rotate the right frame.
