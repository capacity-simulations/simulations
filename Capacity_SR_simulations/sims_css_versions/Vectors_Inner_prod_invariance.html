About This Simulation
This simulation demonstrates that the inner product (dot product) of two vectors is invariant under rotation of the coordinate system.

Two vectors A and B are shown once in a single view with both coordinate systems overlaid: the original frame (x, y) in gray and the rotated frame (x', y') in gold, tilted by angle θ.

Although the components of each vector change when expressed in the rotated frame, the dot product A · B = AxBx + AyBy remains the same in both frames.

Drag the vector tips to reposition them. Use the θ slider to rotate the x', y' axes and grid.
