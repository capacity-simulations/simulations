Physics Explanation

Quantum Result: Particles with spin j deflect to exactly (2j+1) positions, corresponding to mⱼ = -j, -j+1, ..., j-1, j.

Classical Prediction: Random magnetic moment orientations would produce a continuous spread of deflections.

Note: This simulation assumes gj = 1 for simplicity. For silver atoms (original experiment), gj ≈ 2.
