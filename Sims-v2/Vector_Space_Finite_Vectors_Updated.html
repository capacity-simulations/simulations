About the simulation
This interactive explores 2D linear algebra in ℝ². Define vectors a and b, then switch between two modes:

Linear Combination — form v = c₁a + c₂b and see the parallelogram construction.
Dot Product & Projection — explore ⟨a,b⟩, the angle between vectors, and the projection of a onto b.

Drag vector tips on the canvas or edit values in the sidebar. Scroll to zoom in/out.
