About the simulation

This simulation explores an L² inner product space of functions on the real line. You define two Gaussian functions ψ(x) and φ(x), view their linear combination f(x) = c₁ψ + c₂φ, and see how the inner product ⟨ψ|φ⟩, norms, and the angle θ between the “vectors” ψ and φ behave. The shaded area under ψ·φ illustrates the integrand of ⟨ψ|φ⟩. Use the controls to change amplitudes, means, widths, and coefficients to build intuition for function space as a vector space.
