About the simulation

This simulation shows bound-state wavefunctions and probability densities for a particle in a finite square potential well. The well has zero potential between x = −L/2 and x = +L/2 and depth V₀ outside.

Adjust V₀ (well depth) and L (well width) to change the number and spacing of bound states. You can select an energy level to view its wavefunction ψ(x), the probability density |ψ|², and the time-evolving Re[Ψ] and Im[Ψ]. The shaded regions outside the well show tunneling into the classically forbidden zone.
